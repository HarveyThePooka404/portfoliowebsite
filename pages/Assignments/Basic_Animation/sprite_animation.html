04.01.01 Sprite animation on a background

Goal

The assignment was to create a sprite animation (animated with CSS), on a SVG background.


This is an example of the first sketches

Process

The creation of the animation started with a moodboard and characters design. I choose to do two animals (a raccoon and an owl) - which helped for the design of the “Onboarding” project. We then had to create a background, linked to the story. Finally, we linked everything together in the sprite animation.


This is the face done with Illustrator

What worked well

It was a great exercice to learn Illustrator. I was able to reflect what I wanted to express from the sketches to the final Illustrator design.


This is the complete set-up

What could have been improved

I am still not happy with the final design. I was too focused on the technical approach to the project, which led me to overcomplicated the design. I should have done something, in terms of graphic design, a lot simpler, based on my stronger skills of coding. I should have improved on designs that I already know and enjoy (pixel art for example), and not try to push in new territories.

There is also some technical problems on the background that should have been made as a SVG, and not a jpg, to ensure scaling properly.
